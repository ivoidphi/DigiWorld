# ⚡ DIGIWORLD
### *An OOP2 Java 2D RPG Game Project*

> *"You will be transported into a digital world full of elemental Mecha Beasts — fuse with them, create a bond, and battle together."*

---

## 📖 Storyline

You are a **gaming prodigy and 10-time world champion** chosen by the government to beta test a revolutionary new game — **DigiWorld**. Transported into a virtual world, you fuse with powerful creatures called **Mecha Beasts** and battle challengers across three stages.

But not everything is as it seems. Tensions between the lab staff and the General hide a dangerous secret. A mysterious hacker known only as **"The Glitch"** tears apart the digital world — and only you can stop it.

**Will you save DigiWorld… or lose yourself inside it?**

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

- Choose your **Mecha Beast** before each battle
- Each beast has **3 skills** — manage your **mana** and **cooldowns** wisely
- If your beast's HP hits **0**, switch to another — run out of beasts and you **lose**
- Element matchups matter — **strategize** to win
